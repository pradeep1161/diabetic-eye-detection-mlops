Understanding Diabetic Eye Diseases

Diabetic Retinopathy

Damage to retinal blood vessels from high blood sugar.

Macular Edema

Swelling in the macula causing blurred central vision.

Cataracts

Clouding of the eye's lens, common in people with diabetes.

Glaucoma

Increased eye pressure that damages the optic nerve.
